Undereating

The Science of Undereating on the Female Body — Her Body Guide

July 24, 2025

Chronic undereating (low energy availability) can disrupt the hypothalamus and suppress GnRH, leading to irregular or missing periods (functional hypothalamic amenorrhea). It also lowers leptin and thyroid hormones, raises cortisol, and increases bone-loss risk by reducing estrogen, which supports bone formation. Common signs include fatigue, feeling cold, hair shedding, and stress fractures. Restoring adequate calories, dietary fat, and easing excessive training typically allows cycles and bone markers to recover over time.
